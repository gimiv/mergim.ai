1

Define the Core Message

Before generating slides, clearly articulate the primary goal of your presentation. Are you pitching an investment, delivering a progress report, or teaching a concept? Use AI to sharpen your thesis.

Premade Prompt
Act as an expert presentation strategist. I am creating a 10-slide pitch deck for a boutique hotel acquisition. My key selling point is a high Cap Rate (18%) and low operating overhead. Help me write a strong, one-sentence thesis statement and suggest a logical flow for the slides.
2

Generate Slide Content

Once the structure is set, prompt an AI (like ChatGPT or Claude) to act as a copywriter and generate concise, impactful bullet points for each specific slide. Avoid walls of text.

Premade Prompt
Draft the content for a slide titled "Market Opportunity". Provide 3 short, data-driven bullet points explaining why the mid-tier hospitality market in Chicago is currently undervalued. Keep the tone professional and persuasive.
3

Design Automation

Take your generated script and use AI design tools (like Gamma.app, Tome, or Beautiful.ai) to instantly convert the text into visually appealing slides. You can then manually tweak the layouts to match your brand guidelines.
